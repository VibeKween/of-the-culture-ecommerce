Shop Catalog Visual Consistency Guidelines

Image Composition:
  • Product clearly visible and recognizable in thumbnail
  • Consistent scale and framing across all products
  • Model photography maintains professional aesthetic
  • Background consistency (studio vs lifestyle)
Technical Quality:
  • Sharp focus throughout product area
  • Consistent white balance and color temperature
  • Optimized file sizes for fast catalog loading
  • No compression artifacts or noise
Shopping Experience:
  • Products instantly recognizable at thumbnail size
  • Visual hierarchy guides eye through catalog
  • Consistent lighting creates cohesive brand feel
  • Professional presentation builds trust
Brand Standards:
  • Minimal aesthetic maintained throughout
  • Color consistency with brand palette
  • Quality reflects "craft over noise" philosophy
  • Professional model photography standards
