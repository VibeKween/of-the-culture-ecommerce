  • Even lighting without harsh highlights
  • Professional studio lighting aesthetic
Composition & Framing:
  • Product positioning consistent across items
  • Similar scale and proportion in frame
  • Balanced negative space around products
Technical Standards:
  • Sharp focus throughout entire garment
  • No compression artifacts or noise
  • Consistent image quality and sharpness
